Who We Are

Forged in Competition,
Built for Glory

Heaven Esports is India's fastest-growing esports organization, representing elite athletes across multiple competitive titles. Founded with a singular vision — to put India on the global esports map — we scout, train, and elevate the sharpest gaming minds in the country.

Championship DNA

Our teams train under world-class coaches and analysts. With structured boot camps, VOD reviews, and mental conditioning — we breed champions, not participants.

Elite Roster

50+ professional players handpicked from thousands of aspirants. From BGMI riflers to Valorant duelists — our roster is stacked with India's finest talent.

Next-Gen Infrastructure

State-of-the-art gaming facility with high-refresh setups, dedicated coaching rooms, recovery zones, and high-performance nutrition support for our athletes.
